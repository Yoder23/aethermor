# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and evidence-driven community.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Give technical feedback grounded in code, data, and reproducibility.
- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent while maintaining scientific rigor.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of results.
- Bad-faith engagement that blocks collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.
Severe or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the security/contact channel in `SECURITY.md`.
